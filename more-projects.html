Fifty Pence.exe

Fifty Pence

Fifty pence was a service I designed in my second year of my bachelors degree. It is a contactless pad that allowed homeless people to take card donations. The idea for this project came after hearing peoples response "I only have card" when asked for change.

Winner of Fred Maillardet Product Design Breakthrough Award

oODo.exe

oODo

oODo was my final project for my bachelors degree. It was an app that connected customers to their local zero-waste/packaging free shops. It utilised the ability to weigh ingredients specifically for recipes. Then it used a circular reuse system for the packaging used to transport the food.

Nominated for The Waste House Award for Circular Design

Toddler Text Generator.exe

Toddler Text Generator
